My Design Process

My main goal is efficiency.

By beginning to define the problem from multiple perspectives and empathizing to understand user needs, I establish a framework to produce ideas and possible solutions that are supported by quantitative and qualitative research. It is comprised of collaboration, adaptivity, and consideration for accessibility at every point in the design process.

My process was developed through my background of research and data analysis.
